Who am I?

I am Laura Evans, a second year undergraduate student at Oxford Brookes University. I am studying Media, Journalism and Publishing with the intention of going into publishing. For university I live in Oxford but I grew up in Wiltshire. When at home I live five minutes away from the prehistoric monument Stonehenge. I live there with my parents and my brother.

I also have a Sprocker Spaniel called Rosie, she is very cute!
